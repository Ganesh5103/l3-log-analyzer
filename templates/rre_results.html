Understanding RRE Mappings
  • Previous UE Index: The UE index before RRC Re-Establishment (found by CRNTI correlation within 30-second window)
  • CRNTI: Cell Radio Network Temporary Identifier used for correlation
  • PCI: Physical Cell Identifier
  • Cause: The decoded reason for re-establishment failure (reconfigurationFailure, handoverFailure, otherFailure, spare1)
  • Match Timestamp: The exact timestamp where the matching CRNTI was found in the previous UE's journey
